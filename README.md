# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Dashboard (Excel Project) #
## Project Overview ##
This project presents an interactive annual sales dashboard for Vrinda Store, developed using Microsoft Excel. The purpose of the dashboard is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, order trends, and channel performance using structured data visualization techniques.
The dashboard allows users to explore the data dynamically through slicers and pivot-based visualizations.

## Objective ##
The main objectives of this project were:
- To analyze monthly sales and order trends
- To identify top-performing states
- To understand customer demographics (age and gender)
- To evaluate order status distribution
- To assess the contribution of different sales channels
- To build an interactive and user-friendly dashboard in Excel

## Tools and Techniques Used ##
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning and Formatting
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers for interactivity
- Basic Data Analysis
- Dashboard Layout and Design

## Dashboard Components ##
The dashboard includes the following analytical sections:

- Orders vs Sales (Monthly Trend Analysis)
Comparison of total orders and total sales across months to observe performance trends.

- Sales from Top 10 States
Identification of the highest revenue-generating states.

- Gender-wise Order Distribution
Analysis of orders placed by men and women.

- Order Status Breakdown
Distribution of delivered, cancelled, returned, and refunded orders.

- Orders by Age and Gender
Comparison of purchasing behavior across Adult, Senior, and Teenager segments.

- Sales Channel Contribution
Performance comparison of platforms such as Amazon, Myntra, Flipkart, Ajio, Meesho, and others.

- Interactive Filters
Slicers for Category, Month, and Channel to dynamically filter the dashboard.

## Key Insights ##
- Adult customers contribute the highest share of total orders.
- Women customers account for a larger proportion of total purchases compared to men.
- Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra are among the top-performing sales channels.
- The majority of orders are successfully delivered, indicating operational efficiency.
- A small number of states contribute significantly to overall sales revenue.
